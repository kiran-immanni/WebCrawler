Education (CPaCE) Supplemental Application for Transfer Students Using MyCSULB Admissions CSULB Home Admissions Applicant Self-Service Applicant Self-Service Applicant Self-Service is an online service used by CSULB applicants to check the status of their admission application. To access this service, you will need to use your CSULB Campus Identification number to access this service. Your Campus ID is the nine-digit number found in the bottom right-hand corner of all Enrollment Services communications. Please make note of this number as it will be required to access your admission information. Check My Application Status CSULB applicants can check the current status of their admission application online. Just click on the link below to review: The current status of your admission application Documents that may be needed to complete your file Financial Aid Status Information Contact Information currently on file You will be required to enter your CSULB Campus Identification number to access this service. Your Campus ID is the nine-digit number found in the bottom right hand corner of all Enrollment Services communications. Please make note of this number as it will be required to access your admission information. Check My Application Status If you have already been offered admission, you will be directed to the MyCSULB Student Center, or you can go directly to MyCSULB right now, which has information for admitted and current CSULB students. Applicant Email Address Update CSULB applicants are required to maintain a current email address with the University and to check their email regularly because the university uses email as the official means of communication. Verify/Change My Email Address If you have already been admitted or are a current student, you should update your email address via your MyCSULB Student Center. Applicant Financial Aid Status and Forms To check financial aid status and request forms: Log in to Applicant Self-Service Follow the prompts on each screen After requesting an Application Status, do not exit the window Select the Get My Financial Aid Forms button at the bottom of the screen Follow the prompts to request that your forms be sent via email Accept My Waitlist Offer CSULB applicants offered a space on the Admissions Waitlist can accept their waitlist offer by selecting the link below. You will be required to enter your CSULB Campus Identification number to access this service. Your Campus ID is the nine-digit number found in the bottom right hand corner of all Enrollment Services communications. Please make note of this number as it will be required to access your admission information. Accept My Waitlist Offer If you have already been offered admission, you will be directed to the MyCSULB Student Center, or you can go directly to MyCSULB right now, which has information for admitted and current CSULB students.
