enhance the lives of our students, faculty and staff by providing innovative, cost effective, reliable, and meaningful support services for our campus community and partners. By ensuring public safety on and around campus, maintaining and enhancing the university buildings and grounds, promoting fiscal prudence of university resources, attracting, retaining, and educating inspiring university employees, and supporting the work of our auxiliary partners, the Division of Administration and Finance endeavors every day to create a sustainable future for our university and the greater Long Beach community. Auxiliaries of CSULB Auxiliaries provide valuable support to California State University, Long Beach in a number of ways. Forty-Niner Shops, Inc. The Forty-Niner Shops mission is to enhance and support the educational process of the university by providing the goods and services to promote a learning community; by training student employees with life and career skills; and by funding scholarships, internships and other programs that promote student success. Associated Students Inc. (ASI) The mission of Associated Students, Inc. is to improve the quality of campus life for students while enhancing their educational experience. More information at CSULB Research Foundation The CSULB Research Foundation serves the mission of the university by supporting and engaging in research, entrepreneurship, community service, sponsored programs and the acquisition of private resources. CSULB 49er Foundation The mission of CSULB 49er Foundation is to actively promote philanthropy and manage donated resources for the advancement of CSULB.
