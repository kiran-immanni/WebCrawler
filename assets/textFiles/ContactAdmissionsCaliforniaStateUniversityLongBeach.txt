California State University, Long Beach Enrollment Services 1250 Bellflower Blvd. Long Beach, CA 90840 Submit Official Transcripts If you need to submit official transcripts to CSULB, they may be mailed in their original, sealed institution envelope(s) to: California State University, Long Beach Enrollment Services: Admissions 1250 Bellflower Blvd. Long Beach, CA 90840 Electronic copies of official transcripts can be sent directly from the high school, college, or university via a transcript submission service (such as Parchment or College Guidance Initiative) to ES-IDPTrans@csulb.edu. Official transcripts are not accepted via email and cannot be forwarded by the student. Official transcripts can also be dropped off in their original, sealed institution envelope(s) at the Beach Central Intake Windows on the first floor of Brotman Hall. Schedule a Counseling Appointment For more unique questions, CSULB has a knowledgeable team of Admissions Counselors who are available to meet with you virtually to discuss your case. Schedule an Appointment If you have any supporting documents related to your case, please have them ready for your appointment. Plus, check for registration holds, get important deadline reminders, and explore academic support resources.
