You'll have the opportunity to learn and grow from peers, staff and faculty outside of the classroom setting. CSULB's housing options offer students a convenient and supportive living environment. On-campus housing provides students with a sense of community and the opportunity to get involved in campus life. Living on campus can also help students feel more connected to the university and make it easier to access academic and other resources. The Beach Journey Image Living on-campus empowers our students to become more inclusive, responsible, mindful, and engaged community members, embracing their authentic selves at CSULB and beyond. The Beach Journey residential curriculum is an intentional approach to ensuring that learning takes place everywhere, not just in the classroom launched in Fall 2019. Students who to choose to live on campus and become a member of the Housing and Residential Life community will have the opportunity to engage in various strategies that enhance the development and growth of our residents and CSULB community.
