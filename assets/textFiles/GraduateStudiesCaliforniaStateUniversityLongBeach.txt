Program Graduate Tuition & Fees CSULB Home Graduate Studies Graduate Studies Image California State University, Long Beach offers a wide variety of post-baccalaureate certificates, credentials and graduate degrees geared to both full-time and part-time students. CSULB has over 5,500 graduate students across its 123 master’s degree programs, 55 credential programs, 17 post-baccalaureate certificates, and 4 doctoral programs. MISSION: Graduate Studies at California State University, Long Beach is committed to academic excellence and inclusivity through our high-quality, affordable programs. We provide our diverse student body with a graduate education that leads to upward mobility and the opportunity for advancement to fulfill workforce demands, create, innovate, and serve the public good. VISION: Graduate Studies at California State University, Long Beach will empower students to transform themselves and others and prepare graduates to address society's most pressing issues.
