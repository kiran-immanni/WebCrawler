CSULB Home Employment Jobs @ The Beach Founded in 1949, Cal State Long Beach is ranked as one of the top institutions in the country and is the most-applied-to campus of the CSU system. The 322-acre campus is home to nearly 38,000 students and boasts over 365,000 Beach alumni.
